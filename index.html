Frequently Asked Questions

Everything Austin pool owners ask us before getting started. Can't find your answer? Give us a call — we love talking pools.

How often should I have my pool serviced?

For best performance and water quality, we recommend weekly service. Austin's heat, pollen, and Live Oak debris make weekly maintenance essential to keep your water safe, your equipment running smoothly, and to catch small issues before they become costly repairs.

What does a typical weekly visit include?

Our weekly service includes chemical balancing, skimming the surface, brushing the walls and steps, emptying skimmer and pump baskets, checking filter pressure, and a visual inspection of all pool equipment.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No, we operate on a month-to-month basis. We believe in earning your business every week with excellent service, not locking you into a long-term contract.

What if my pool turns green?

If you're on our Full Service or Resort Living plan, algae treatments are included at no extra charge. If you're not a customer, we offer standalone Green Pool Recovery services to turn it around fast.

Stop spending Saturday mornings testing chemicals and skimming leaves. Let your local Austin pool experts handle it — so you can just enjoy the splash.
